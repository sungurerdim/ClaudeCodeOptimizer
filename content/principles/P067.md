**Severity**: Critical

**Why**: Ensures all claims are backed by objective evidence, preventing false confidence

**Enforcement**: Skills required - verification_protocol, test_first

**Project Types**: all

**Rules**:
- **Command Output Required**: Completion claims must include command output
- **No Uncertainty**: Avoid uncertainty language
